How It Works

The Pomodoro technique is a method to increase your focus on work and tasks within in a certain timeframe, traditionally 25 minutes in length, rewarded by short breaks.

These 25 minute intervals of focus are usually done in groups of 3-4, including short breaks in-between, with a long break to watch funny cat videos after the final interval.
